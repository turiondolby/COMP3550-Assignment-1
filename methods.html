What are the Water Sanitation methods?

Methods of Sanitation

There are various methods of sanitizing water the details of which can be seen below.



1. CHLORINATION

This method of purification is one of the most effective methods in cleaning water. It involves the addition of Chlorine to water to kill various bacteria. This is a chemical process.Chlorine damages the cell membrane and then inhibits its respiratory and DNA functions. Unfortunately this method does not remove the solid particles from the water.



2. DISTILLATION

This method of purification is the most common. It utalises a condenser and a vaporizer. It requires water to be boiled then condensed by changing its state from gaseous to liquid. This is an example of a mechanical process which only requires water to change its state.



3. FILTRATION

This was the method used until the development of distillation and chlorination. It is the process where suspended particles are removed from water. However bacteria may still be present because they are micro organisms that are too small to be retained by the filter. Filtration is a simple process where polluted water is passed through a porous material which retains the solid material. The porous material may be ceramic, charcoal or granular activated carbon.
